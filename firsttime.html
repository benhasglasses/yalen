THE STORY & WORLD

Step right up! Enter the vibrant, slightly chaotic world of the **Starlight Circus**, a traveling show steeped in tradition and brimming with exuberant energy. This is a place where gravity is optional and rules are merely suggestions, but for one young man, it's the prison of his own expectations.

Meet **[Protagonist's Name]**, the introverted, bookish son of the circus manager. He’s more comfortable backstage with ledgers than under the big top, dreaming of a life far from the greasepaint and glittering costumes. His quiet existence is about to get a dazzling, dual-threat shake-up.

When the circus’s two most flamboyant and utterly captivating chorus girls—the grounded, witty **JANE** and the mercurial, dramatic **VERONICA**—find themselves unexpectedly vying for his attention, [Protagonist's Name]'s world is turned upside down. They are everything he is not: bold, glamorous, and utterly magnetic.

Our shy hero is thrust into the spotlight of his own life, forced to choose between the safety of the sidelines and the thrilling, terrifying act of **true love**. In a world where every day is an adventure, he’s about to discover that for love, there’s always a first time for everything.
