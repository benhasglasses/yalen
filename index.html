READY TO HAVE YOUR WEAK SONG REPAIRED BUT FEARFUL OF THE PROCESS?

RELAX! HERE’S HOW IT WORKS:

PY Songs International and Song Saviors Ltd. are dedicated to relieving the extreme anxiety experienced by many of our composer clients who arrive at our clinic to have their ailing songs surgically made over.

Our concept is based on recognizing and taking steps to reduce the Terror, Utter Disgust, Stress and Horror (hereafter referred to as TUSH) associated with a composer's visit to a song doctor.

Recognizing TUSH in a composer client:

Common signs include: a tense face, snarling or growling. In severe cases, we may see crouched posture, hissing and ears pinned down to the side.

Stressors:

Examples include noise, odor, pain, erroneous repetition and odor. Our goal is to reduce such stress for optimal song treatment.

Reducing TUSH:

The song doctor speaks in a calm, quiet voice. Rewards such as treats, toys, or petting are used during diagnostic exams.

Advanced Procedures:

For key changes or deleting a verse, a towel wrap, muzzle, or Elizabethan collar may be needed. Sedatives or calming pheromones are available.
